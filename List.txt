Types of List :
1. Ordered List 
2. Unordered List
3. Definition/Description List

Structure

Ordered List:
	<ol>
		<li> Content </li>
	</ol>
Un-ordered List:
	<ul>
		<li> Content </li>
	</ul>
Definition/decription List:
	<dl>
		<dt> Definition Term </dt>
		<dd> Definition Description </dd>
	</dl>
